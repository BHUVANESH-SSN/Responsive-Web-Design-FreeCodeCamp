Shiv Nadar ‑ Visionary Entrepreneur & Philanthropist

Shiv Nadar is an Indian industrialist and philanthropist best known as the founder of HCL Technologies, a global IT services giant. Born in 1945 in Tamil Nadu, he turned a garage startup into a global tech powerhouse.

  • Founded HCL Technologies in 1976; now serves 60+ countries :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
  • Started the Shiv Nadar Foundation in 1994, championing transformative education
  • Established SSN College of Engineering & Shiv Nadar University
  • Awarded Padma Bhushan in 2008 for contributions to industry :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Named among Forbes' most powerful & philanthropic Indians
  • Invested US $1.1 billion in education, impacting 36,000+ students :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

His lifelong mission is to build an equitable future by empowering the next generation through quality education and innovation.
